About the Program

What is the
Inbound Exchange Program?

Keimyung University maintains exchange agreements with 247+ partner institutions worldwide. Students nominated by their home university can study at KMU for one semester or one academic year, earning credits transferable back to their home institution.

A wide range of courses are available in English and Korean across 10 colleges including Humanities, Social Sciences, Engineering, Arts, and Medicine. Structured dormitory support and a comprehensive orientation program ensure a smooth transition.
